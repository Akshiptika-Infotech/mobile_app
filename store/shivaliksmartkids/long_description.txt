Shivalik Smart Kids is the official school companion app that brings your child's
entire school life into one secure, easy-to-use place. From fee payments and
attendance to exam results, timetables and instant notices, everything you need
is just a tap away.

Built for the whole school community — parents, students, teachers, drivers,
security staff and the front office — Shivalik Smart Kids keeps everyone
connected and informed in real time.

★ FOR PARENTS
• View fee dues and pay securely online in seconds
• Download and save fee receipts as PDF
• Track your child's daily attendance
• See exam marks, grades and report cards
• Follow the school calendar, holidays and events
• Receive instant push notifications for notices and reminders

★ FOR STUDENTS
• Check fees, receipts and payment history
• View exam results and progress
• See the class timetable at a glance
• Track school transport and route details
• Manage your profile and ID information

★ FOR TEACHERS
• Mark daily attendance quickly
• Enter and submit exam marks and grades
• View class timetables and the academic calendar
• Manage exams and assessments on the go

★ FOR SCHOOL STAFF
• Drivers: view routes, student lists and trip attendance
• Security: log gate entry/exit, visitors and gate passes
• Reception: manage visitors, calls and front-desk activity
• Admins & clerks: dashboards, student records, fee collection and reports

★ WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT
• Fast, clean and simple — designed for everyday use
• Secure login with private, role-based access
• Real-time updates so you never miss an announcement
• Works smoothly on phones and tablets
• Modern Material design with light and dark themes
